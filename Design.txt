Based on This: http://www.kongregate.com/games/mofunzone/noidzor
It's breakout, but with skills, more balls, and maybe vs / co-op.

Needs:
Blocks have health
Blocks need to have a "team" so friendly balls pass through them.
Balls need to have a limit on how many bounces they survive for. (Maybe a max damage that they can deal?)
Regen time on creating new balls

Stats:
Off: Increases damage balls deal
Def: Decreases damage taken, increases health of blocks
Endurance: Increases health, increases max damage a ball can deal
Speed: Decreases time until next ball is summoned. Also decreases cooldowns on skills
Magic: Increases max mana and mana regen
